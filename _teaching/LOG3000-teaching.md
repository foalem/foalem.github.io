Instructor for the **LOG3000 Software Engineering Process** course.
Topics covered include the fundamentals of software engineering processes, the phases of the software lifecycle, development methodologies, requirements engineering, design, implementation, testing, configuration management, and project management.  
The course also introduces methods for process representation, empirical analysis of software processes, and techniques for process improvement.
